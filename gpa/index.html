GPA Calculation Rules (Ilia State University)

1. Formula: GPA = Σ(Course GP × Credits) / Σ(Total Credits)
2. GP Scale:
- F (0-50): 0.00 GP
- E (51-60): 1.00 GP
- D to A (61-95): GP increases linearly by 1/12 (~0.083) for each point above 60.
- A+ (96-100): 4.00 GP
3. Rounding: Final GPA is rounded to two decimal places.
